About Gabrielle M.Sagot

Gabrielle Sagot is a graduate student in Economics at Ecole Polytechnique (France) currently visiting at Stockholm University (SOFI) until August with Anne Boschini. Her interests lie in gender inequalities, especially on the labour market, with both quantitative sociology and economics approaches. She has previously worked on women underrepresentation in the field of research in Economics in France using an innovative dataset on PhDs.
